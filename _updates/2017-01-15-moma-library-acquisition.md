American Psycho and AutoSummarize are now part of <a href="https://www.moma.org/">MoMA</a>'s permanent collection through the <a href="https://soulellis.com/entries/lotpw.html">Library of the Printed Web</a>, acquired by The Museum of Modern Art Library. The collection includes 244 items by 130 artists curated by Paul Soulellis.
