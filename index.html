Ceremony & Reception

We are getting hitched on Pebble Beach in Brooklyn Bridge Park at 6pm (enter the park on Washington & Plymouth Streets). Dinner, drinks & dancing to follow at the DUMBO Loft, three blocks from the park. Dress to impress in bright colors and metallics.

Where to stay

The Marriott, Aloft, Sheraton, and Nu Hotel are the closest hotels to the wedding. They are all walking distance from the ceremony, but car services are also available through the hotels. You can also try AirBnB, which can put you closest to the reception and ceremony.

Airports

Fly into La Guardia (LGA) or John F. Kennedy (JFK). But avoid Newark Airport (EWR).

How did it all begin?

It started on the corner of 5th Avenue & Douglass Street in Brooklyn, NY. The neighborhood – Park Slope. It was the late summer of 2007 and Carly was walking home from a dinner with good friends and Jason was on a bro-walk with his long-distance, high school, internet-friend Joey. They met on the sidewalk, shook hands, gave each other googly eyes, and thus began CARLY + JASON FOREVER.

Since they met, Carly went from being a studio manager for an artist to a grad student in the School of Visual Arts' Department of Photography, Video & Related Media to starting her own professional photography business. Since they met, Jason went from being a graphic designer at an investment firm to a grad student in Rhode Island School of Design's Digital + Media program to a product designer at Etsy. Together they have: flown to Portland six times; flown to Atlanta eight times; flown to Florida five times; driven over 3000 miles in Carly's 1996 Honda Accord through Yellowstone, the Badlands and the Oregon Trail; adopted a street cat named Penny; and jumped out of a perfectly good plane at an altitude of 11,000 feet celebrating their five-year anniversary.

Last Thanksgiving (Carly's favorite holiday), Jason + Carly were in Portland visiting family. Unbeknownst to Carly, Jason's mama had given him a gorgeous family ring. Hugging in front of a not-at-all-cliché roaring fire, Jason said to Carly, "Hey, let's do something crazy." "Like what?" "LIKE GET MARRIED." And now, we're asking you to come celebrate with us, watch us get hitched, drink drinks, sing songs, make toasts, cry, laugh and dance 'til you can't dance no more. See ya there!
