Jason Huff lives and works in Brooklyn, NY. His works explore our relationship to technology with language, everyday objects, and the internet. His projects are included in the Library of the Printed Web and in the Special Collections at the Whitney Museum of American Art. In 2012 Traumawien Press published a limited edition of his collaborative text-based work, American Psycho.
